Departments

The departments at Cambridge Hospital are equipped with state-of-the-art facilities and staffed by a team of
dedicated professionals.

  • Cardiology

  • Neurology

  • Hepatology

  • Pediatrics

The Cardiology department at Cambridge Hospital specializes in diagnosing and treating heart conditions, providing advanced cardiovascular care with a patient- centered approach. Their expert cardiologists utilize cutting-edge techniques and technology to promote heart health and improve patient outcomes.

The Neurology department at Cambridge Hospital offers specialized care for neurological disorders, providing comprehensive evaluation, diagnosis, and treatment for conditions affecting the brain, spinal cord, and nervous system. Their team of neurologists is dedicated to delivering personalized care and innovative therapies to enhance the quality of life for their patients.

The Hepatology department at Cambridge Hospital focuses on the diagnosis and management of liver diseases, including viral hepatitis, liver cirrhosis, and liver cancer. Their experienced hepatologists employ advanced diagnostic techniques and therapeutic interventions to provide comprehensive care for patients with liver-related condition.

The Pediatrics department at Cambridge Hospital specializes in providing comprehensive healthcare services for infants, children, and adolescents. Their team of pediatricians is dedicated to promoting the health and well-being of young patients through preventive care, vaccinations, and the diagnosis and treatment of childhood illnesses. They strive to create a comfortable and child-friendly environment.
